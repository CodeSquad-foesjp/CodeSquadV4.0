About

CodeSquad V4.0 is an inter university problem solving coding competition organized by the CENSOC - Computer Engineering Society, Faculty of Engineering, University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Code Squad scopes over a wide range of computer science domains to practice different programming paradigms as functional programming. CodeSquad was initiated with the aim of polishing the programming skills of the undergraduates while inspiring and exposing them towards the arena of internationally based programming competitions. CodeSquad will be the best platform for you to stimulate your thirst for programming challenges and to be competitive.

The competition involves solving creative problems, all within a constrained time period of 2 hours for selection round and 6 hours for final round. Selection round of the competition will be conducted via online and final round of the competition will be conducted as a physical event. All the competitors are given the freedom to refer any resource except AI during the solving process. Tasks should be completed within given time.

How to Register

  • Form your 3 member Squad
  • create a HackerRank account under the name of the team
  • Fill out the Registration Form and Provide the HackerRank account in the Form
  • All done. We will see you in the Battlefield.

Rules

  • The competition will be held in two stages, the selection round within 2 hours and the final round within 6 hours.
  • This is a team competition , Each team should consist 3 Undergraduates of any University in Sri Lanka.
  • Only the HackerRank account that you provided in registrations must be used in the competition by all three members.
  • You cannot change your three members during or after the first round of the tournament.
  • Among the registered teams , 15 teams will be selected based on the final score of the team. Score will be given according to the 'HackerRank'.
  • A team is allowed to skip a question if they desire, during the solving.
  • Team ranking is done according to ‘HackerRank’ and every team will be given a live screen that indicates their current position.
  • The winning team is selected which has solved the maximum number of questions within the given time in efficient manner.
  • Final round will be held physicaly at Faculty of Engineering University of Sri Jayewardenepura premises.
  • In any case moderators have authority to take a viva for further clarification to avoid plagiarism. All the participant should follow the moderators' decisions.
  • No plagiarism will be tolerated.
  • During Final Round Usage of AI is Strictly Prohibited and if caught, team will be disqualified.
  • All participants should stick to the given time.
  • No extra time will be given at any cost even if you are late.
  • Decisions of the moderators will be the final and you have to agree to these decisions at all times.
